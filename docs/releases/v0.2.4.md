# Lyra v0.2.4 — Profiles + Virtual Audio Cable

This release adds two big operator features on top of the v0.2.3 transmit
line: **operator Profiles** (named TX/RX chain bundles) and a built-in
**Virtual Audio Cable (VAC)** path for digital-mode software (MSHV, WSJT-X,
JTDX, fldigi, …) — no TCI audio required.

Bench-verified on a Hermes Lite 2+: FT8 RX decodes clean through VAC, and
TX on the air via VAC (worked KF2OG, N8SDR spotted coast-to-coast on
PSKReporter).

## New

- **Virtual Audio Cable (VAC).** Route radio RX audio to a PC audio cable
  for your digital-mode app to decode, and capture the app's TX audio from a
  second cable straight into the transmitter. Settings → Audio has the VAC1
  panel (enable, auto-enable-for-digital, output/input devices, RX/TX gain).
  To transmit through VAC, set **Settings → TX → Mic source = "PC Soundcard
  (VAC1)"** — TCI can still handle PTT/CAT/frequency while audio rides VAC.
- **Operator Profiles.** Save your whole TX/RX signal chain — bandwidth +
  lock + filter low edge, mic source, mic gain/boost, TX drive, TCI gains,
  AGC mode, auto-mute-on-TX, TX safety timeout, and the VAC source — as a
  named profile. Recall from the front **Profile** dock or **Settings →
  Profiles**; Save offers overwrite-active or save-as-new. Profiles are
  **mode-agnostic** with **per-family auto-recall**: switching to a digital
  mode recalls your digital chain without ever changing your operating mode
  or sideband. (A digital/VAC profile flips mic source + VAC enable as a
  unit; a TCI profile keeps TCI.)
- **Auto-start on launch** opt-out (Settings → Hardware). On by default;
  untick to have Lyra load without connecting and click Start yourself.
- **Hardware radio list**: add a radio by manual IP and remove radios.

## Fixed

- Operator TX bandwidth now reaches the TX channel (was stuck at the
  channel default ~3 kHz regardless of the selected filter).
- Settings export/import no longer carries the radio connection identity
  (which could hang on a shared/imported profile).

## Install

Download **Lyra-Setup-0.2.4.exe** and run it. Windows 10 (1809+) / 11,
64-bit. FFTW WISDOM is generated by the app on first launch.

## Hardware / scope

Hermes Lite 2 / 2+ over HPSDR Protocol 1. RX, plus SSB and digital
(TCI **and** VAC) transmit.
